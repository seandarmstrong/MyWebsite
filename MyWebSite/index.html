Coding and Programming

I have been dabbling in coding and programming for several years, playing with languages such as HTML, CSS, Python, and some Ruby. These were mainly free online tutorials as I had time and as the mood struck me. Then, after taking a year off to travel the world I decided to get serious about programming and enrolled in a course through Education 2 Go, an online program for certifications and skills training, that focused on the Java language. The Beginner and Intermediate classes I took sealed my fate and I knew I wanted to get serious about it and make a career change. Enter Grand Circus.

Long-Term Travel

In November 2016 I left my job in the marketing and advertising field in preperation for a year long journey. On January 6, 2017 I boarded a plane bound for Costa Rica where I would live for the next 6 months teaching English and traveling around that beautiful country.

Hometown

I hail from Beer City, USA aka Grand Rapids, MI. Grand Rapids was a bit of a sleepy town some 15 years ago but as really come into its own, offering a plethora of things to do throughout the year. Traveling the world was amazing but it felt really good to come back to the city I call home.
